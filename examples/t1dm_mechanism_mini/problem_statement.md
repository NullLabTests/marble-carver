# Problem statement: Type 1 Diabetes mechanism (mini)

> **Date:** 2025-12-01
> **Author:** marble-carver demo
> **Domain:** Medicine / Immunology

---

## The marble

Type 1 diabetes (T1DM) results from autoimmune destruction of pancreatic β
cells. The triggers and precise sequence of events are unknown. Known
associations include HLA haplotypes, viral infections (especially enterovirus),
and molecular mimicry. But a unified mechanism remains elusive.

## The angel

A specific molecular mechanism linking enteroviral infection, HLA risk alleles,
and β-cell destruction — or a definitive falsification of the molecular
mimicry hypothesis.

## Why this matters

T1DM incidence is rising 3-5% annually. Prevention strategies require knowing
the causal mechanism. Current immunotherapy is blunt (global
immunosuppression).

## Definition of done

A documented, evidence-supported causal chain:
Virus → Molecular mimicry → Cross-reactive T cells → β-cell death
or an equally specific alternative, with citations verified for every step.

## Initial gaps

- [ ] Which enteroviral epitopes structurally mimic β-cell autoantigens?
- [ ] Do T cells from newly-diagnosed T1DM patients cross-react with those
      epitopes?
